World-Class Support

Need help? Our friendly support team is available to assist you during normal working hours (9AM - 5PM) on weekdays. Simply send us an email or chat with us on WhatsApp for assistance. We typically respond within a few hours.

Best Value Guaranteed

Foneo is committed to bringing you unmatched value on refurbished smartphones. If you see the same refurbished or pre-owned device, in the same condition, and the equivalent 12-month limited warranty at a better price within 7 days of your purchase, on any other South African website or retail store, we will send you a discount voucher for the difference, plus free delivery anywhere in South Africa.

Easy Returns & Exchanges
