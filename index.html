Frequently Asked Questions

A few sales-related topics people often ask about.

How long will it take to receive my order?

In most cases, we typically complete a new order within 5-8 working days. You can click here to check your order status. We do not offer Express or Next Day delivery at this time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all recognized payment methods including Visa/Mastercard (cheque, debit or credit card), Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT), cash on delivery or manual bank/ATM deposit. Choose any option you prefer during checkout.

Is my personal information safe?

We take online security very seriously. When you use our service, all your details are encrypted using an SSL certificate so your information is 100% safe.

How do I contact your support team directly?

We are available via email, WhatsApp, Facebook and Instagram. You can contact us anytime and we'll get back to you within 1-2 working days. Our working hours are from 9AM till 5PM on weekdays.

Is waterproofing maintained with refurbished devices?

Please note that waterproofing is NOT maintained on refurbished products. As part of the refurbishing method which is based on indsutry specifications and best practices, the devices are opened in order to carry out tests on the product before they are sold. As a result, waterproof sealing from the original manufacturer is NOT guaranteed on refurbished products sold on the Foneo platform.
